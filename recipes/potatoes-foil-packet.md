# Ingedients {.recipe-ingredients}

* Potatoes
* Onion
* Oil
* Rosemary
* Garlic

# Directions {.recipe-directions}

1. Slice potatoes and onions and toss together with olive oil
2. Place onto foil with rosemary and garlic
3. Pop onto some ready coals
